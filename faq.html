Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is a retail fulfillment method where a store doesn't keep the products it sells in stock. Instead, when a store sells a product, it purchases the item from a third party and has it shipped directly to the customer.

2. How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout. Typically, orders are delivered within 7-14 business days.

3. What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most products. Please visit our Return Policy page for more details.
